General Admission Requirements

Meet the following requirements to join our ICT programs

Certificate Programs

Minimum Requirements:

  • KCSE Grade D+ (Plus) or equivalent
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Age 18 years and above
  • Valid national ID or passport

Duration:

6 months to 1 year

Available Programs:

  • Certificate in ICT
  • Certificate in Computer Applications
  • Certificate in Web Design
  • Certificate in Hardware Maintenance

Diploma Programs

Minimum Requirements:

  • KCSE Grade C- (Minus) or equivalent
  • Mathematics Grade D+ or above
  • English Grade D+ or above
  • Valid national ID or passport

Duration:

2-3 years

Available Programs:

  • Diploma in ICT
  • Diploma in Software Engineering
  • Diploma in Network Administration
  • Diploma in Computerized Accounting

Professional Certifications

KASNEB Certified Courses:

  • KCSE Grade C (Plain) or equivalent
  • Mathematics Grade C- or above
  • English Grade C- or above

KNEC Certified Courses:

  • KCSE Grade D+ or equivalent
  • Relevant subject requirements

Available Certifications:

  • CPA (Computerized Accounting)
  • DICT (Diploma in ICT)
  • CDACC Certified Programs

Program-Specific Requirements

Additional requirements for specialized programs

Software Programming

Prerequisites:

  • Basic mathematics skills
  • Logical thinking ability
  • Computer literacy
  • Problem-solving skills

Recommended Background:

  • Mathematics or Computer Studies in KCSE
  • Previous programming experience (optional)
  • Interest in technology

Network Administration

Prerequisites:

  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Understanding of computer systems
  • Technical aptitude
  • Attention to detail

Recommended Background:

  • Physics or Computer Studies
  • Hardware troubleshooting experience
  • Interest in network technologies

Computerized Accounting

Prerequisites:

  • Mathematics Grade C- or above
  • Basic accounting principles
  • Computer literacy
  • Numerical skills

Recommended Background:

  • Business Studies or Accounting
  • Commerce background
  • Interest in financial systems

International Students Requirements

Additional requirements for students from other countries

Documentation

  • Valid passport
  • Student visa (where applicable)
  • Academic transcripts (certified)
  • Certificate equivalency verification
  • Birth certificate
  • Police clearance certificate

Language Proficiency

  • English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Minimum IELTS score: 5.5
  • Minimum TOEFL score: 550 (paper) / 213 (computer)
  • Alternative: English proficiency interview
  • Exemption for native English speakers

Financial Requirements

  • Proof of financial support
  • Bank statements (6 months)
  • Scholarship documentation (if applicable)
  • Sponsor guarantee letter
  • Medical insurance coverage

Health Requirements

  • Medical examination report
  • Vaccination certificates
  • HIV/AIDS test certificate
  • Tuberculosis screening
  • Health insurance coverage

Application Tips

Helpful advice to strengthen your application

Apply Early

Submit your application well before the deadline to ensure processing time and avoid last-minute issues.

Complete Documentation

Ensure all required documents are properly certified, translated (if necessary), and submitted in the correct format.

Professional Presentation

Present yourself professionally in all communications and ensure your personal statement reflects your goals clearly.

Stay in Contact

Maintain communication with the admissions office and respond promptly to any requests for additional information.
