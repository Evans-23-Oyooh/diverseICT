Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, undergraduate programs require KCSE C+ or equivalent, while postgraduate programs require a relevant bachelor's degree.

When do applications open?

Applications for the next academic year typically open in January and close in May. Check our admissions page for specific dates.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer various scholarships based on academic merit, financial need, and special talents. Contact our financial aid office for more information.

Can I visit the campus?

Absolutely! We offer campus tours Monday through Friday. Please contact our admissions office to schedule a visit.
