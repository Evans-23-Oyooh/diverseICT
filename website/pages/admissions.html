Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission

1

Choose Your Program

Browse our academic programs and select the one that aligns with your career goals and interests.

View Programs
2

Check Requirements

Review the specific admission requirements for your chosen program, including academic qualifications and prerequisites.

View Requirements
3

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including transcripts, certificates, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

4

Submit Application

Complete and submit your online application form along with all supporting documents and application fee.

5

Application Review

Our admissions committee will review your application. This process typically takes 2-4 weeks.

6

Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision via email. If accepted, you'll receive enrollment instructions.

Required Documents

Ensure you have all necessary documents before applying

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed application form
  • KCSE certificate or equivalent
  • Official transcripts
  • Birth certificate
  • National ID or passport copy
  • Passport-size photographs (2)
  • Application fee receipt

Postgraduate Programs

  • Completed application form
  • Bachelor's degree certificate
  • Official undergraduate transcripts
  • Two recommendation letters
  • Personal statement/research proposal
  • CV/Resume
  • English proficiency test (if applicable)
  • Application fee receipt

International Students

  • All documents above (as applicable)
  • Passport copy
  • Visa application documents
  • English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Financial support documents
  • Medical examination report
  • Police clearance certificate
