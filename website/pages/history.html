Founders' Vision

The story behind our mission to transform ICT education

A Vision Born from Necessity

In 2010, Kenya was experiencing rapid technological growth, but there was a significant gap between industry demands and the skills available in the job market. Our founders, Dr. Sarah Wanjiku and Prof. James Mwangi, both experienced ICT professionals and educators, recognized this challenge.

Having worked in various tech companies and witnessed firsthand the struggle to find qualified ICT professionals, they decided to establish an institution that would bridge this gap. Their vision was simple yet ambitious: create a college that would provide practical, industry-relevant ICT education accessible to all Kenyans.

"We didn't just want to teach technology; we wanted to empower individuals to become innovators and problem-solvers who could contribute meaningfully to Kenya's digital transformation."

- Dr. Sarah Wanjiku, Co-Founder

Starting with limited resources but unlimited passion, they began with a small classroom and a handful of computers. Their commitment to quality education, practical training, and student success has been the driving force behind our growth and success.

Our Legacy & Impact

Transforming lives and communities through ICT education

Career Transformation

Over 85% of our graduates have secured employment or started their own businesses within 6 months of graduation, with average salary increases of 150%.

Innovation Hub

Our students have developed over 200 innovative projects, with 15 startups launched from our Innovation Hub, creating over 300 jobs in the tech sector.

Community Outreach

Through our community programs, we've provided free basic computer literacy training to over 2,000 individuals in underserved communities.

Women in Tech

45% of our graduates are women, contributing to closing the gender gap in technology. Our Women in Tech program has supported over 500 female students.

Regional Influence

Our graduates work in leading tech companies across East Africa, with many holding senior positions and contributing to the region's digital transformation.

Excellence Recognition

Consistently ranked among the top 5 ICT training institutions in Kenya, with recognition from government bodies and industry associations.

Looking Forward

Our commitment to continued excellence and innovation

The Next Chapter

As we look to the future, Diverse ICT College remains committed to staying at the forefront of technological education. Our strategic plan for 2025-2030 includes:

  • Expansion to 5 additional campuses across East Africa
  • Launch of degree programs in partnership with international universities
  • Development of AI and Machine Learning specialization tracks
  • Establishment of a research and development center
  • Creation of 10,000 new job opportunities through our graduates
  • Launch of free online courses for underserved communities

We remain dedicated to our founding principles: accessibility, quality, and practical relevance. As technology continues to evolve, so do we, ensuring our students are always prepared for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow.
